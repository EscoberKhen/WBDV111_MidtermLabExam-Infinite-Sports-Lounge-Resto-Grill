About Us

Infinite Sports Lounge & Rest Grill is a casual dining and entertainment venue designed for people who want to relax, enjoy great food, have refreshing drinks, and spend quality time with friends and family in a comfortable and lively atmosphere. Our space is created for guests who want more than just a meal we provide a place where good food, great company, and entertainment come together. Whether you're here to catch the big game on our screens, celebrate a birthday or special occasion, hang out with friends, or simply unwind after a long and busy day, Infinite Sports Lounge & Rest Grill offers the perfect setting. With a welcoming environment and friendly service, we aim to make every visit enjoyable and memorable for our guests. At Infinite Sports Lounge & Rest Grill, we bring together the exciting energy of a sports bar, the flavorful dishes of a resto grill, and the relaxing vibe of a lounge. The result is a unique place where people can eat, watch, celebrate, and unwind all in one vibrant and welcoming destination.
